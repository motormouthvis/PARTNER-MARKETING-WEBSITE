The Basics

What exactly is Dream Neighborhood?
Dream Neighborhood is a neighborhood data tool that embeds directly on your real estate website. When a buyer views a listing, they can instantly explore local demographics, schools, safety, commute times, walkability, points of interest and more — without ever leaving your site.
What data and insights does it include?
More than 38 hyperlocal data points per area, including population and household makeup, schools and ratings, safety scores, walkability and mobility, commute times, nearby restaurants, parks and shopping, plus housing-market context — far more depth than what general portals surface.
Is the explorer limited to one address or listing?
Not at all. The explorer isn’t locked to a single address — buyers can search and explore any neighborhood across the entire United States, for free. They can compare commute times, schools, safety, and lifestyle across as many areas as they like, all without leaving your site.
How is this different from Zillow, Realtor.com, or Redfin?
The portals have neighborhood data — but it lives on their websites, surrounded by competing listings and other agents. Dream Neighborhood puts that context directly on your website, so buyers stay engaged with you instead of clicking away to your competition.
Is the data fair-housing compliant?
Yes. We’re careful to exclude protected-class demographics from the experience. The insights focus on lifestyle, amenities, schools, safety scores, and mobility — context that helps buyers without crossing fair-housing lines.

Setup & Technical

How difficult is it to install?
Not difficult at all. It’s one line of code that you or your web developer paste into your website once — that’s it, about as simple as adding a tracking pixel. We also install it for you for free and are happy to help if you get stuck.
Will it work with my current website?
Yes. It works with virtually any platform — WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, Webflow, custom-built sites, and the major IDX providers. If you’re unsure, just ask us during a demo.
Will you install it for me?
Absolutely — we’ll install Dream Neighborhood on your website for free. Book a quick call and we’ll handle the setup, or walk your developer through it.
What if my website is managed by a developer or IDX provider?
No problem. We provide one line of code your developer can add in minutes, and we’re happy to jump on a quick call with them directly to make installation as smooth as possible.
Does it slow down my website?
No. The script is lightweight and loads asynchronously, so it won’t affect your page-load speed or your SEO.
How does it know which neighborhood to show?
On a listing page it automatically detects the property address and centers the neighborhood explorer there. Buyers can also search any other area they’re curious about.
Popup or embedded — which should I use?
The popup is our flagship: one line of code adds a neighborhood button to every listing, perfect for keeping your current design. The embedded explorer drops a full-width, interactive panel directly into a page when you want neighborhood data front and center. You can use either, or both.

Leads & Features

Can I capture leads with it?
Yes. Turn on the optional built-in lead form and buyers enter their name, email, and phone to unlock the full neighborhood report — turning high-intent browsers into contacts.
Where do the leads go?
Captured leads land straight in your inbox and dashboard, so you can follow up right away or push them into your CRM.
Can I share neighborhood reports outside my website?
Yes. Generate a private link to any neighborhood explorer and share it by text, email, or social post — great before a showing so buyers arrive informed, or as a follow-up afterward. You can also require a lead form on shared links to turn a single social post into many captured leads.
Can I generate branded PDF reports?
Yes. Export a polished, branded PDF of the full neighborhood breakdown for any address — perfect for buyer packets, listing presentations, and follow-ups.
Can I use it on my phone at a showing?
Definitely. Pull the explorer up on your phone or tablet and walk buyers through schools, lifestyle spots, and commute times live while you’re standing in front of the home.

Brokerages & Teams

Can I roll it out across my whole brokerage?
Yes. Invite your whole team to a shared account — no per-seat licenses. Pricing scales on views and reports, not users, so you can grow your team without renegotiating seats.
Can I control what agents can do?
Yes. Decide whether agents can build their own explorers and reports or only use brokerage-wide templates you create, so your brand stays consistent.
Do I get analytics across my team?
Yes. See which agents generate the most leads, which explorers get the most views, and where engagement is coming from — all from one dashboard.

Data & Sources
