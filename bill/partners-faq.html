Partner Questions

Everything IDX providers, website platforms, agencies, and brokerages ask about partnering with Dream Neighborhood. Still have questions? Email support@dreamneighborhood.com.

The Partner Program

What is the Dream Neighborhood partner program?
It’s a way for IDX providers, website platforms, agencies, and large brokerages to add comprehensive neighborhood data to every listing across the sites they power — as a native or white-labeled feature — and earn recurring revenue while doing it.
Who is the program for?
IDX providers and real estate website builders, web-development agencies serving real estate clients, large brokerages and teams, MLSs and real estate networks, and referral partners with relationships across the industry.
What partnership models do you offer?
Four flexible models: Development Partners embed us natively into their platform; Agency Partners offer it to the clients they serve; Referral Partners earn recurring revenue from introductions; and Enterprise Partners run custom, large-scale deployments. We’ll help you pick the best fit.
How is this different from the standard product?
The product is the same powerful neighborhood data — but as a partner you can deploy it across many sites at once, white-label it, set exclusive pricing for your clients, and share in the recurring revenue rather than just buying a single subscription.

Integration & Technical

How do we integrate Dream Neighborhood?
In most cases it’s a single line of code added to your platform or client websites. For deeper integrations we can work with your team directly — and we handle the setup for free.
Can we deploy it across all of our client sites at once?
Yes. With one platform-level integration the experience can roll out across your whole portfolio, and new client sites activate automatically — no per-site setup and nothing to maintain as you grow.
Does it work across different IDX platforms?
Yes. Dream Neighborhood works across the major IDX providers and website builders — we’re already live on sites running IDX Broker, iHomefinder, and AeroIDX Pro, among others.
Can we white-label or co-brand it?
Yes. Deliver the entire experience under your own brand — your logo, colors, and domain. Your clients see your product; we power it behind the scenes.
Will it slow down our clients’ sites?
No. The script is lightweight and loads asynchronously, so it has no meaningful impact on page-load speed or SEO.
How long does it take to launch?
Most partners go from install to launch in days, not months. Our team handles the heavy lifting so there’s almost zero lift for you or your clients.
Who manages the data and updates?
We do. Dream Neighborhood owns the data pipeline, hosting, and ongoing updates — so the experience stays current with no maintenance burden on you.

Revenue & Commercial

How does revenue share work?
You earn recurring revenue from every active deployment among your clients. The more sites that go live, the more you earn — month after month. We’ll share the specific terms when we talk.
Are there minimums, upfront fees, or setup costs?
None. There are no minimums, no upfront fees, and no setup costs — you earn when your clients pay.
Can we offer exclusive or discounted pricing to our clients?
Yes. Partners can offer exclusive, discounted pricing to their clients that isn’t available on our public plans — a great way to drive adoption.
Can we bundle it into our product or plans?
Absolutely. Many partners make Dream Neighborhood a standard, built-in feature of their platform or premium tiers — adding value that helps win and retain customers.
Who handles billing and subscriptions?
We do. Dream Neighborhood manages billing, subscriptions, and payments, so you don’t have to build or operate any of it.

Onboarding & Enablement

Can we offer free trials to our clients?
Yes. You can offer your clients free trials so they can experience the value before committing.
Are there introductory offers or coupons?
Yes. We can provide introductory discount offers and coupons to help you convert clients and accelerate adoption.
Do you provide demo or example sites?
Yes. We can stand up example deployments so you can show clients exactly what the experience looks like on a real listing.
Do you provide marketing and sales support?
Yes. We’ll work with you on messaging, demos, and materials to help your team promote Dream Neighborhood to your customer base.

Support & Operations

Who supports our clients?
We provide timely support for the Dream Neighborhood experience, so the support load doesn’t fall on your team. You can stay as hands-on or hands-off as you like.
What analytics do partners get?
Partners get visibility into usage and lead activity across their deployments — which sites drive the most engagement and where leads are coming from.
What are the contract terms?
Terms are flexible and partner-friendly, with reasonable notice periods on both sides — no heavy long-term lock-in. We’ll walk you through the full agreement before you commit.
What happens to client subscriptions if the partnership ends?
Client subscriptions remain with Dream Neighborhood, so the experience your clients rely on keeps running without disruption.

Data & Compliance

What data is included?
More than 38 hyperlocal data points per area — demographics, schools, safety, walkability and mobility, commute times, points of interest, and housing-market context.
Where does the data come from?
Best-in-class sources including the U.S. Census Bureau, Redfin, Walk Score, NCES, and Google Maps — combined into our proprietary neighborhood database.
Is it fair-housing compliant?
Yes. We exclude protected-class demographics and focus on lifestyle, amenities, schools, safety scores, and mobility — context that helps buyers without crossing fair-housing lines.
