Dream Neighborhood + RealtyCandy partnership announcement

We are excited to officially announce our new partnership with RealtyCandy. Through this partnership, RealtyCandy users can now offer buyers a richer neighborhood discovery experience directly on their websites with Dream Neighborhood’s interactive Neighborhood Explorer.

What buyers expect now

Today’s buyers want more than listings. They want context:

  • Schools
  • Demographics
  • Commute insights
  • Walkability
  • Market trends
  • Local lifestyle information

The challenge

Most buyers leave agent websites to search for that information elsewhere.

What changes with Dream Neighborhood

Dream Neighborhood changes that. With a lightweight integration, RealtyCandy users can now keep buyers engaged directly on their own sites while creating a more modern, high-converting search experience.

Why this partnership made sense

This partnership made sense because both companies believe the future of real estate websites should be:

  • More engaging
  • More informative
  • More brand-driven
  • Less dependent on third-party portals

We are incredibly excited about what this unlocks for RealtyCandy clients, and this is only the beginning.
