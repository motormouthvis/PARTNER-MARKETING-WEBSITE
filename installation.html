INSTALLATION

Install Dream Neighborhood in One Line of Code

Add rich neighborhood intelligence to every listing page in under 5 minutes. No redesign. No per-page edits.

Works with any website or IDX platform.

TWO WAYS TO INSTALL

Pick the Method That Fits Your Site

Most sites use the popup widget. Use the embedded explorer when you want the full Neighborhood Explorer built into a specific page.

Popup Widget

RECOMMENDED

The widget appears as a clean chat bubble in the bottom right corner on every listing and neighborhood page. 

<script src='https://app.dreamneighborhood.com/explorer/sdk.js' async></script>

Paste this single line into the <head> section of your site. The widget will automatically appear on your approved URL.

Embedded Explorer

Display the full interactive Neighborhood Explorer directly on any page of your site. 

<div id="dn-explorer" data-widget-number="2"></div>
<script src="https://app.dreamneighborhood.com/explorer/inline.js" async></script>

Use this method if you want to embed the full explorer on specific pages.

HOW INSTALLATION WORKS

Live in Three Simple Steps

From subscription to a working widget, the whole process takes just a few minutes.

1

Subscribe and provide your URL

Sign up for Dream Neighborhood and tell us the permitted URL where the widget should run.

2

Paste the one-line code

Add the single line of code to your approved site, no developer headaches and no redesign.

3

The widget activates automatically

Once your URL is approved, the widget switches on across your listing and neighborhood pages.
